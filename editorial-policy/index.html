Editorial Guidelines

Effective Date: 2026-01-05

1. Independence and Objectivity

YUNHAI prides itself on analytical independence. Our journalists and contributors are required to maintain strict objectivity, ensuring that data-driven insights remain uninfluenced by outside commercial interests.

2. Fact-Checking and Verification

Every report and feature article undergoes a rigorous fact-checking process. We cite credible institutional sources, global trade organizations, and verified corporate data to ensure the reliability of our reporting.

3. Sponsored and Partner Content

To support our global operations, we occasionally collaborate with partners to publish "Partner Content" or "Sponsored Insights." We maintain full transparency by clearly labeling such content. All sponsored material must still adhere to our professional quality standards.

4. Corrections Policy

Integrity requires accountability. If a significant factual error is identified, we will issue a correction promptly within the article and provide a clear explanation of the change.
