David Vance

Based in New York

David Vance leads the retail and consumer behavior desk at The Commerce Review. With over 15 years of experience in financial journalism and market analysis, David specializes in the digital transformation of legacy brands and the rapid evolution of Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) ecosystems in North America.

Before joining The Commerce Review, David served as a senior market strategist for a leading Wall Street consultancy, where he advised Fortune 500 retail conglomerates on omnichannel integration and supply chain resilience.
